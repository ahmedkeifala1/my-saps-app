# Bank and Institution Logos

This directory contains the official logos for all Card Switch and Instant Payment Service participants.

## Required Logo Files

### Card Switch Participants (12 banks)

- `slcb-logo.png` - Sierra Leone Commercial Bank (SLCB)
- `rcb-logo.png` - Rokel Commercial Bank (RCB)
- `gtb-logo.png` - Guarantee Trust Bank (GTB)
- `access-bank-logo.png` - Access Bank
- `ecobank-logo.png` - Ecobank
- `zenith-bank-logo.png` - Zenith Bank
- `sky-bank-logo.png` - Sky Bank
- `bloom-bank-logo.png` - Bloom Bank
- `uba-logo.png` - United Bank for Africa (UBA)
- `vista-bank-logo.png` - Vista Bank
- `fbsl-logo.png` - First Bank of Sierra Leone (FBSL)
- `cmb-logo.png` - Commerce and Mortgage Bank (CMB)

### Instant Payment Service Participants (Mobile Money)

- `orange-money-logo.png` - Orange Money
- `africell-money-logo.png` - Africell Money
- `qmoney-logo.png` - QMoney

## Logo Specifications

- **Format**: PNG with transparent background (preferred) or JPEG
- **Size**: 200x100 pixels (minimum) or larger
- **AspectRatio**: Preferably maintain bank's official logo proportions
- **File Naming**: Use lowercase with hyphens (e.g., `slcb-logo.png`)

## How They're Used

The logos are displayed on the Participants page:

- Card Switch Participants section
- Instant Payment Service Participants section

If a logo file is missing or fails to load, the participant's abbreviation will display as a fallback (e.g., "SLCB", "GTB", "UBA").

## Adding Logos

Simply place the PNG/JPEG files in this directory with the exact filenames specified above. They will automatically be used on the Participants page.
